The Best Movies According to Ogulcan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Vinland Saga

One of my all-time favorite animes is Vinland Saga, a masterpiece that dives deep into themes of revenge, freedom, and personal growth, set against a gritty Viking backdrop.

Batman The Dark Knight

Another work I truly admire is The Dark Knight, directed by the legendary Christopher Nolan, where Heath Ledger delivers an unforgettable and haunting performance as the Joker, redefining the character forever.

Interstellar

Interstellar, also directed by the brilliant Christopher Nolan, is a breathtaking sci-fi epic that explores love, time, and human survival in the most profound and visually stunning ways.
